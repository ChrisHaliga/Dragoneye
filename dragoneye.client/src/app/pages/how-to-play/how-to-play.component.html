Dragoneye

A medieval fantasy TTRPG that evolves across three distinct eras

What is This Game?

Dragoneye is a medieval fantasy TTRPG where you play heroes navigating warring kingdoms (Chaos), bureaucratic guild systems (Order), or industrial corporate dystopia (Ascension). Everything revolves around seven elements: Earth (Geo), Water (Hyd), Fire (Pyr), Air (Æro), Light (Lux), Dark (Nyx), and Arcana (Arc).

This is a "roleplaying game" and roleplaying always comes before game.

What Makes This Different

  • This system prioritizes storytelling and character development over complex mechanics
  • Card-based resolution means you'll never need to memorize dozens of rules - everything you need is written on your cards
  • Simple multiplicative math replaces complicated modifiers and calculations
  • The seven-element framework creates intuitive relationships that are easy to learn and remember
  • Roleplaying drives the narrative, with mechanics supporting your character's story rather than constraining it

Essential Concepts Everyone Needs

  • Roleplay First: Character personality and background drive decisions more than mechanical optimization
  • Card-Based Mechanics: Action and resolution cards contain all rules needed for specific situations
  • Element Interactions: Seven-element relationships affect combat, magic, and story themes
  • Character Domains: Specialization paths that guide character growth and ability selection
  • Collaborative Storytelling: Everyone contributes to creating memorable shared experiences

What You Need to Play

  • 3-6 players (including one Game Master)
  • 3 D6 dice required for dark sorcery effects and the 7 resolution cards
  • Digital Option: Use our website's integrated tools including digital character sheets, hex-grid maps, skill tree cards, automatic language translation, and in-game chat features
  • Physical Option: Everything can be printed including character sheets, maps, cards, and even printable D6s
  • 2-4 hours per session

The Seven-Element System

The elements form the foundation of everything in this world:

  • Physical Elements: Earth (Geo) beats Water (Hyd) beats Fire (Pyr) beats Air (Æro) beats Earth
  • Light (Lux): Beats Fire, Air, and Arcane
  • Dark (Nyx): Beats Water, Earth, and Arcane
  • Arcane (Arc): Beats all Physical elements (Earth, Water, Fire, Air)

All character races, attributes, continents, and action types connect to these seven elements. Your character's elemental associations shape their strengths, weaknesses, and story role.

How Play Works

  1. Describe what your character wants to do based on their personality, training, and abilities
  2. Skill checks, combat, and other challenges are resolved using action/resolution cards plus your character's stat bonuses
  3. Success comes from clever thinking, good roleplay, and using your character's strengths
  4. Dice are only rolled for dark sorcery - whether you're casting it or being targeted by it

First Session Prep Checklist

  • All players have completed characters with both stats and roleplay pages filled
  • GM has prepared initial scenario with clear character motivations
  • 3 D6 dice available for dark sorcery encounters
  • Digital tools set up OR printed materials ready
  • 2-4 hours blocked out for play
