Design Philosophy

Strategic Depth

Every choice matters, but complexity serves gameplay rather than overwhelming players.

Collaborative Play

Mechanics encourage teamwork and shared storytelling rather than solo optimization.

Meaningful Randomness

Chance creates interesting situations, but player skill and preparation are always relevant.

Narrative Integration

All mechanics support and enhance storytelling rather than interrupting it.
