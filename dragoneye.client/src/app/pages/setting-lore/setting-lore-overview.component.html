World Highlights

Aether Crystals

Magical crystals that power both spells and mechanical devices, creating a unique magitech civilization.

The Sundering

A catastrophic magical event 500 years ago that reshaped the world and created the current political landscape.

The Ironlands

Industrial city-states powered by magical engines, representing the fusion of magic and technology.

The Card Codex

The mysterious source of all Card System magic, with cards appearing to those destined to wield them.

Current Events

Rising Tensions

Political conflicts emerge between the industrial Ironlands and traditional kingdoms as resources become scarce.

Magical Storms

Strange magical weather patterns appear without warning, disrupting travel and commerce across the continent.

Ancient Ruins

Newly discovered ruins reveal secrets about the Sundering and hint at lost technologies from the Age of Harmony.

Setting Design Philosophy

Familiar Yet Unique

Classic fantasy elements with distinctive magical and technological twists that create fresh experiences.

Living World

The world changes and evolves, with current events and consequences that respond to player actions.

Rich Backstory

Deep history provides context and adventure hooks without overwhelming players with required reading.

Adventure Potential

Every location and historical event is designed to generate interesting stories and conflicts.
