Design Philosophy

Distinct Identity

Each race and domain should feel mechanically and narratively unique.

Balanced Options

No choice is objectively better; each offers different strengths and gameplay styles.

Narrative Integration

Mechanical choices connect to rich cultural and personal storytelling opportunities.

Growth Potential

Characters evolve and gain new capabilities throughout their adventures.
